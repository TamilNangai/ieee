About the Conference

The 2026 International Conference on Innovations in Emerging Technologies for Sustainable Development (ICIETSD) scheduled on April 9–10, 2026 aims to bring together researchers, academicians, and industry experts to share innovations and ideas in sustainable and emerging technologies.

The event fosters cross-disciplinary collaboration in fields like computing, communication, infrastructure, energy, environment, and security to drive a more sustainable future.

This conference is technically sponsored by IEEE and provides an excellent platform for knowledge exchange, networking, and showcasing groundbreaking research in sustainable technologies.

Our Goals

Encourage Innovation

Encourage innovation in areas like AI, Blockchain, IoT, and 5G to address global challenges across sectors like energy, environment, digital infrastructure and security.

Support Sustainable Solutions

Support sustainable solutions in infrastructure, energy, and manufacturing through eco-friendly and low-carbon technology development.

Advance Security & Digitalisation

Advance cybersecurity, digitalisation, and smart systems to create secure, efficient, and connected technological ecosystems.

Enable Collaboration

Enable collaboration between academia, industry, and research communities to drive innovation and knowledge sharing for sustainable development.
